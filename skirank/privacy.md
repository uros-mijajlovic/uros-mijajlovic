# Privacy Policy for SkiRank

**Last updated: September 10, 2025**

This Privacy Policy describes how **SkiRank** ("we," "us," or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application, SkiRank (the "App").

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy is designed to explain what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it. Please read this carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your information and how we will treat it.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Skiing and Activity Data
When you use SkiRank, we collect various types of data related to your skiing activities:

- **Location Data**: GPS coordinates, ski runs taken, lift locations, resort areas
- **Ski Session Information**: Session duration, distance skied, timestamps, vertical feet
- **Skiing Metrics**: Speed, elevation changes, run difficulty, skiing behavior
- **Resort Data**: Ski resort identification, lift usage, trail information
- **Usage Patterns**: Frequency of ski sessions, preferred runs, skiing times

### How We Use Your Skiing Data
- To analyze your skiing patterns and provide personalized insights
- To generate rankings, statistics, and analytics about your ski sessions
- To improve our analytics algorithms (in anonymized and aggregated form)
- To provide recommendations and insights about your skiing habits

### Data Collection Methods
SkiRank collects data through:
- GPS and location services on your device
- Motion sensors and activity tracking on your device (if available)
- Manual input from you
- Integration with ski resort systems (if applicable)

## 2. Data Storage and Retention
- Skiing data is stored securely to provide you with historical analytics and trends
- Location data is retained to generate run analysis and session history
- Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained longer for service improvement
- You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us

## 3. How We Share Your Information
We do not sell your personal skiing data. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **Service providers**: With third-party vendors who help us operate the App, process data, or provide analytics services
- **Legal requirements**: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety
- **Business transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to users)
- **Anonymized data**: We may share aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify you individually

We do not share your specific location data or detailed skiing patterns with third parties except as necessary for service operation or as required by law.

## 4. Location Data
SkiRank requires access to your location data to function properly. We collect location information:
- While you are skiing or using the App
- To track ski runs and routes
- To provide location-based analytics

You can control location permissions through your device settings, but disabling location services will limit the App's functionality.

## 5. Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:
- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Secure storage of location and skiing data
- Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
- Regular security assessments and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices
You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request access to the personal information and skiing data we hold about you
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your skiing data and personal information
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate information
- **Opt-out**: Disable location tracking or data collection through device settings
- **Export**: Request a copy of your skiing data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us at urosmijajlovic@gmail.com

## 7. Children's Privacy
SkiRank is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information immediately.

## 8. Third-Party Services
SkiRank may use third-party services for:

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

## 9. International Data Transfers
Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country.

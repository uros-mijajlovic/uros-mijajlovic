# Privacy Policy for KissDrop

**Last updated: September 10, 2025**

This Privacy Policy describes how **KissDrop** ("we," "us," or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application, KissDrop (the "App").

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy is designed to explain what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it. Please read this carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your information and how we will treat it.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Photos and Biometric Data
When you use KissDrop to create a personalized kiss design, we collect:
- **Photos you upload or take**: We process photos of your face to analyze your lip features
- **Biometric data**: We analyze facial features, specifically lip shape and characteristics, to generate your personalized design
- **Generated designs**: We temporarily store the kiss designs created based on your photos

### How We Use Your Photos and Biometric Data
- To analyze your lip features and create personalized kiss designs
- To generate products featuring your design when you make a purchase
- To improve our design generation algorithms (in anonymized form)

### Data Retention
- Photos are stored temporarily during the design generation process
- Photos are typically deleted after design generation is complete, unless you choose to save your design or make a purchase
- If you make a purchase, we may retain your photo and design data for order fulfillment purposes
- Design data may be retained for a reasonable period to allow you to reorder products

## 2. Product Purchase Information
When you purchase products through KissDrop, we collect:
- **Shipping information**: Name, address, and contact details necessary for order fulfillment
- **Payment information**: Processed securely through third-party payment processors (we do not store full payment details)
- **Order history**: Information about products you have purchased

## 3. How We Share Your Information
We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:
- **Service providers**: With third-party vendors who help us operate the App, process payments, or fulfill orders
- **Legal requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights
- **Business transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to users)

We do not share your photos or biometric data with third parties except as necessary for order fulfillment or as required by law.

## 4. Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:
- Secure storage of photos and biometric data
- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Your Rights and Choices
You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request access to the personal information we hold about you
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your photos and personal data
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate information
- **Opt-out**: Choose not to save your designs or photos after use

To exercise these rights, please contact us at urosmijajlovic@gmail.com

## 6. Children's Privacy
KissDrop is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information or photos, we will take steps to delete such information immediately.

# Privacy Policy for TripRank

**Last updated: September 10, 2025**

This Privacy Policy describes how **TripRank** ("we," "us," or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application, TripRank (the "App").

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy is designed to explain what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it. Please read this carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your information and how we will treat it.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Driving and Vehicle Data
When you use TripRank, we collect various types of data related to your vehicle and driving activities:

- **Location Data**: GPS coordinates, routes taken, trip start and end locations
- **Trip Information**: Trip duration, distance traveled, timestamps
- **Driving Metrics**: Speed, acceleration, braking patterns, driving behavior
- **Vehicle Data**: Vehicle identification, mileage, fuel consumption (if available)
- **Usage Patterns**: Frequency of trips, preferred routes, driving times

### How We Use Your Driving Data
- To analyze your driving patterns and provide personalized insights
- To generate rankings, statistics, and analytics about your trips
- To improve our analytics algorithms (in anonymized and aggregated form)
- To provide recommendations and insights about your driving habits

### Data Collection Methods
TripRank collects data through:
- GPS and location services on your device
- Vehicle sensors and connected vehicle systems (if available)
- Manual input from you
- Integration with vehicle telematics systems (if applicable)

## 2. Data Storage and Retention
- Driving data is stored securely to provide you with historical analytics and trends
- Location data is retained to generate route analysis and trip history
- Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained longer for service improvement
- You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us

## 3. How We Share Your Information
We do not sell your personal driving data. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **Service providers**: With third-party vendors who help us operate the App, process data, or provide analytics services
- **Legal requirements**: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety
- **Business transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to users)
- **Anonymized data**: We may share aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify you individually

We do not share your specific location data or detailed driving patterns with third parties except as necessary for service operation or as required by law.

## 4. Location Data
TripRank requires access to your location data to function properly. We collect location information:
- While you are driving or using the App
- To track trips and routes
- To provide location-based analytics

You can control location permissions through your device settings, but disabling location services will limit the App's functionality.

## 5. Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:
- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Secure storage of location and driving data
- Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
- Regular security assessments and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices
You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request access to the personal information and driving data we hold about you
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your driving data and personal information
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate information
- **Opt-out**: Disable location tracking or data collection through device settings
- **Export**: Request a copy of your driving data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us at urosmijajlovic@gmail.com

## 7. Children's Privacy
TripRank is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information immediately.

## 9. International Data Transfers
